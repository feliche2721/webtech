About Us

Welcome to Purr n' Snouts!

At Purr n' Snouts, we believe in nurturing the special bond between pets and their owners. Our mission is to provide top-notch services that enhance the health, happiness, and well-being of your furry friends. With a deep passion for animals and a commitment to excellence, we strive to be your trusted partner in pet care.

Our Story

Founded by dedicated pet lovers, Purr n' Snouts was born out of a genuine love for animals and a desire to offer comprehensive care solutions for pets. We understand that every pet is unique and deserves personalized attention and care. Our journey began with a simple goal: to create a haven where pets feel loved, cared for, and safe.

Our Commitment

Our Commitment At Purr n' Snouts, we are dedicated to delivering the highest standard of care. Our team comprises experienced professionals who are passionate about animal welfare and continuously strive to improve our services. We believe in the power of positive reinforcement, a nurturing environment, and tailored care plans to ensure that every pet thrives.

Our Services

Training Classes

  • Our training classes are designed to build a harmonious relationship between you and your pet.
  • We offer a variety of classes tailored to different skill levels:
    • Basic obedience training for puppies
    • Advanced behavioral techniques for older dogs
  • Our experienced trainers use positive reinforcement methods to ensure effective and enjoyable learning.
  • By the end of the course, you'll have a well-behaved pet who is a joy to be around, both at home and in public.

Pet Care

  • We ensure your furry friends receive the love, attention, and care they deserve with our comprehensive pet care services.
  • Services included:
    • Daily feeding and exercise routines tailored to each pet's needs.
    • Specialized attention for pets with medical needs to ensure their health and well-being.
    • Regular grooming and hygiene maintenance.
    • Playtime and mental stimulation activities.
    • Administering medications and monitoring health conditions.
    • Behavioral training and socialization sessions.
    • Emergency care and on-call veterinary services.
  • Our team is dedicated to maintaining the well-being and happiness of your pets while you're away.
  • Giving you peace of mind that your pets are in good hands.

Furr-ever Bath

  • Luxurious grooming experience designed to keep your pet looking and feeling their best.
  • Use of high-quality, pet-friendly products to ensure a gentle yet thorough clean.
  • Each bath includes:
    • Full wash
    • Coat conditioning
    • Ear cleaning
    • Nail trimming
  • Skilled groomers who pay special attention to each pet's specific needs.
  • Ensuring a stress-free and enjoyable experience for your pet.

MedVet Service

  • Top-notch veterinary care ensuring your pet's health and well-being.
  • Services include:
    • Routine check-ups
    • Vaccinations

  • Compassionate veterinarians committed to delivering the highest standard of care.
