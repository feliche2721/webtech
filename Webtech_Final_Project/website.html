About Us

Welcome to Purr n' Snouts!

At Purr n' Snouts, we believe in nurturing the special bond between pets and their owners. Our mission is to provide top-notch services that enhance the health, happiness, and well-being of your furry friends. With a deep passion for animals and a commitment to excellence, we strive to be your trusted partner in pet care.

Our Story

Founded by dedicated pet lovers, Purr n' Snouts was born out of a genuine love for animals and a desire to offer comprehensive care solutions for pets. We understand that every pet is unique and deserves personalized attention and care. Our journey began with a simple goal: to create a haven where pets feel loved, cared for, and safe.

Our Commitment

Our Commitment At Purr n' Snouts, we are dedicated to delivering the highest standard of care. Our team comprises experienced professionals who are passionate about animal welfare and continuously strive to improve our services. We believe in the power of positive reinforcement, a nurturing environment, and tailored care plans to ensure that every pet thrives.

Our Services

Training Classes

Our training classes are designed to build a harmonious relationship between you and your pet. We offer a variety of classes tailored to different skill levels, from basic obedience training for puppies to advanced behavioral techniques for older dogs. Our experienced trainers use positive reinforcement methods to ensure that your pet not only learns effectively but also enjoys the process. By the end of the course, you'll have a well-behaved pet who is a joy to be around, whether at home or out in public.

Pet Care

We ensure your furry friends receive the love, attention, and care they deserve with our comprehensive pet care services. From daily feeding and exercise to specialized attention for pets with medical needs, we provide a safe and nurturing environment. Our team is dedicated to maintaining the well-being and happiness of your pets while you're away, giving you peace of mind that they are in good hands..

Furr-ever Bath

We offer a luxurious grooming experience with our Furr Bath service, designed to keep your pet looking and feeling their best. We use high-quality, pet-friendly products to ensure a gentle yet thorough clean. Each bath includes a full wash, coat conditioning, ear cleaning, and nail trimming. Our skilled groomers pay special attention to each pet's specific needs, ensuring a stress-free and enjoyable experience.

MedVet Service

Our MedVet Services provide top-notch veterinary care, ensuring your pet's health and well-being. From routine check-ups and vaccinations to emergency care and surgical procedures, our state-of-the-art facility is equipped to handle all your pet's medical needs. Our compassionate veterinarians are committed to delivering the highest standard of care, using the latest medical techniques and technology.

Safe Hedge

Safe Hedge is our exclusive service designed to provide a secure and enriching environment for your pet. Whether you need pet-proofing solutions for your home or secure outdoor spaces, we offer customized setups that ensure your pet's safety. Our experts assess your living space and implement measures to prevent accidents and escapes, giving you peace of mind while allowing your pet to explore and play freely.
